Welcome to the Promo brand ambassador documentation. Here you'll find guides and resources to help you find work and manage your profile.

## What you can do

Promo connects you with agencies looking for brand ambassadors:

- **Browse activations** — Find job opportunities that match your skills and location
- **Apply with one click** — Submit applications quickly with your saved profile
- **Manage your profile** — Keep your resume, experience, and media portfolio up to date
- **Track your shifts** — View upcoming and past shifts in one place
- **Check in to shifts** — Confirm your attendance with GPS-verified check-ins
- **Get paid** — Track invoices and payment status for completed work
